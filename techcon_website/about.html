History

Founded in 2017 by a collective of tech professionals and educators, TECHCON began as a small meetup in a San Francisco co-working space.

The goal was simple: create a collaborative hub for those building the future.

Over the years, TECHCON has evolved into one of the most anticipated global conferences in the tech calendar â€” drawing over 10,000 attendees from more than 50 countries annually.

Mission

At TECHCON, we believe that innovation should be inclusive, collaborative, and ethical. Our mission is to:

  • Empower developers, leaders, and visionaries across industries
  • Connect diverse voices and perspectives to drive responsible tech development
  • Inspire solutions that solve global challenges and improve lives

We're not just talking about the future. We're building it - together.
